Course content

The course is designed to aim at imparting a basic level of programming to the individual. After completing the course, the individual is able to use the computer for basic purposes of preparing his personnel/business letters, troubleshooting, and installing windows and software this allows a common individual to be also a part of the computer users list by making them digitally literate. This helps small business communities, maintain their small account using computers and enjoy the world of Information Technology.
